About Us

S. E. S. Polytechnic, Solapur

Solapur Education Society founded in 1931 and has continuously progressed in the field of Education. It has a reputation of developing academic and moral character of student community. The society started in the field of technical education by starting S. E. S. Polytechnic in 1984.
