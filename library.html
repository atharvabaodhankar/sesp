Facilities

Central Library

The library functions from 10.00 a.m. to 05.30 p.m. on all working days. All the books have been classified as per Dewey decimal classification and are available for open access.

SESP Library provides services & facilities to the requirements of the Institutionals teaching & programmes. It has an excellent collection of reference books prescribed by MSBTE.

Library Rules & Regulations :

  1. Library will remain open on all working days from 10:00 AM to 5:30 PM
  2. Silence should be strictly observed in the library.
  3. Identity card is essential to enter in the library and to borrow learning material.
  4. Use of mobile phone is strictly banned in the library.
  5. Uniform is compulsory for all students on all days. No student is allowed in the library without uniform.
  6. Rare books, encyclopedia, dictionary, IS code, current issues of journals etc will not be permitted to take outside. These materials will be available for reference in the library only.
  7. Before borrowing the books any damages to the book should be pointed out immediately to the issue counter, otherwise the student shall be responsible for the same.
  8. Do not use Pen drive and other external devices in the e-library.
  9. Return the books within seven days from borrow date otherwise dues will be charged Rs. 1 /- Per day per book.
  10. Tobacco chewing and spitting are strictly prohibited.
  11. Ex-student and personalities from industry are eligible for casual membership on Principal's permission only.
  12. Principal / Librarian reserves the right to suspend membership of any student if he / she is found guilty. Misbehavior in the library or disturbing library discipline may also lead to suspension of the membership.
