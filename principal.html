Shri A. A. Bhawtankar

M. E. Civil Engineering

Dear Students and Parents,

Welcome to the Solapur Education Society's Polytechnic, a vibrant community rooted in strong human values. Our vision is to mold you into not just skilled engineers but responsible citizens who can make a positive impact on the world.

At S.E.S. Polytechnic, we are committed to providing exceptional education that prepares you for success in industry and entrepreneurship. Our graduates have consistently demonstrated their capabilities, with many holding leadership positions in top companies like Tata Motors, Bajaj, Mahindra & Mahindra, L&T, Precision Camshafts, and Kirloskar Ferrous. These industry leaders regularly recruit directly from our campus.

We invite you to join our family and embark on a journey of skill development and empowerment. We look forward to celebrating your future successes.

Best wishes,
A. A. Bhawtankar
